Privacy Policy

TRC Call to Action 92 — Auto Sales Training

Effective date: August 20, 2025

This course is delivered by Brooke Ashley Group and hosted using GitHub Pages and Netlify. We respect your privacy and explain here what data we collect, why, and how to contact us.

What we collect

  • Learner details: name and optional email you enter for your certificate.
  • Completion data: exam score, pass/fail status, certificate ID, timestamp, and industry (“Auto Sales”).
  • Lead forms: if you complete a quote/bulk access form, we collect the fields you submit (e.g., name, work email, company, rooftops, message).
  • On‑device data: the course stores your progress in your own browser via localStorage so you can continue later. This is not sent to our servers unless you submit the exam.

How we use your data

  • Issue and validate your certificate when you pass (≥70%).
  • Track completions for organizations (aggregate reporting).
  • Respond to sales and support requests you send.

Where data is stored

  • Netlify Forms: Exam submissions and lead forms are processed and stored by Netlify in data centers they operate. We export records for reporting to client organizations if requested.
  • Your device: Course progress is kept in your browser (localStorage) until you clear it or hit “Reset Course”.

Retention

Unless a client requires a different term, we keep completion records for 24 months to support audit needs, then delete them. You can request deletion sooner (see “Your rights”).

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share completion records with the employer or organization that enrolled you, and with service providers (e.g., Netlify) who process data on our behalf.

Security

The site is served over HTTPS by Netlify. Certificates are generated client‑side in your browser as PDFs. Do not share your certificate file publicly if it contains personal details you wish to keep private.

Your rights

If you are in Canada, you can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information. Contact us at replace-this@email with your name, the certificate ID (if available), and the site URL.

Children

This training is intended for adults and not for children under 16.

Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted here with a new effective date.
